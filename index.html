About

I'm a developer with a passion for creating technology that's genuinely helpful and accessible to everyone. My journey in tech started with curiosity about how things work and evolved into a mission to build products that make people's lives easier.

During my time as a Data Engineering intern, I learned the importance of building scalable, reliable systems. Working with large datasets and cloud infrastructure taught me to think about performance, efficiency, and the broader impact of technical decisions.

"The best technology is invisible—it just works, making complex things feel simple and empowering people to do what they need to do."

Currently, I co-founded a startup with my brother, Assistive Technologies, focused on building technological solutions for the disabled. We're creating tools that bridge the gap between technology and the people who need it most. This journey has taught me that the best solutions come from truly understanding user needs and iterating based on real feedback.

I believe in building with purpose—whether it's a mobile app that helps someone navigate their day independently or a data pipeline that surfaces insights that drive better decisions. Every project is an opportunity to make technology more human.
